Korean Royal Cooking: Mushroom Hotpot

This recipe was given to me in Korean at the Hansol Culinary Academy in Jongno South Korea. I made a vegetarian version in class and will share how I did that here.

Ingredients Needed

For the the Hotpot

  • King Oyster Mushroom: half of one
  • Shiitake Mushrooms: two
  • Oyster Mushrooms: fourth of a pack
  • Mushrooms: fourth of a container
  • White Cabbage: two leaves
  • Carrot: 1/6th of one
  • Minari: two stalks
  • Onion: one fourth
  • Crown Daisy: one stalk
  • Tofu: 1/8th of a block

For the Broth

  • Daikon Radish: 1/10th of one
  • Onion:1/8th of one
  • Leeks Stem: 2cm of a stalk
  • Leek Leaves: one stalk
  • Korean Soy Sauce for Soup

For the Dipping Sauce

  • Soy Sauce: 2 big spoons full
  • Sugar: 1 big spoon full
  • Vinegar: 1 big spoon full
  • Broth (from the Hotpot): 4 big spoons full
  • Cracked/Ground Seasame Seeds: one small spoon full
  • Brown Mustard or Wasabi: add to taste
